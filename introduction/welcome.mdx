This course was built by someone who failed his first Python class — and went on to spend over a decade building AI software professionally. That experience shapes everything here: the tools you'll use, the workflow you'll develop, and the way concepts are taught. You won't just learn syntax; you'll learn how to actually work as a Python developer in the AI era.

## Your instructor

Hi everyone, I'm Ahamed Bilal, and I'll be guiding you through the world of Python and Artificial Intelligence.

My journey with Python began in 2022 when I first became interested in programming and AI. Like many beginners, I started by watching random YouTube tutorials and trying out different courses online. At first, I was confused by many concepts and often struggled to understand how everything connected together.

Over time, I learned that the key to mastering Python is not watching endless tutorials but practicing consistently and building real projects. In this series, I'll share what I've learned and help you learn Python in a simple, practical, and beginner-friendly way.

## What makes this course different

Most Python courses teach you syntax and stop there. This course teaches you two equally important things:

**1. The syntax** — how to write Python code. Python is genuinely easy here; it reads almost like plain English, and AI assistants can help fill any gaps you encounter.

**2. Your workflow** — the tools you use, how you set them up, how you start projects, save files, debug code, and structure your work. This is the part most courses skip entirely, and it's the part that determines whether you actually become productive.

When you understand your workflow, your learning accelerates dramatically. Add modern AI tools into the mix, and you'll progress faster than any generation of developers before you.
