## Get started in four steps

New here? Follow these steps in order to get the most out of the course.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Meet your instructor">
    Head to the [Welcome](/introduction/welcome) page to learn about Dave Ebbelaar — 10+ years of Python experience, 30,000+ students taught, and a decade running AI software projects. Understanding his background and teaching philosophy will help you get the most from the material.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Install Python">
    Follow the [Getting Started](/getting-started/what-is-python) guide to install Python and VS Code on your machine. You'll set up the same professional workflow used at real AI companies — not a simplified toy environment you'll have to unlearn later.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Learn the basics">
    Work through the [Python Basics](/basics/intro-to-programming) section to build a solid foundation. Variables, data types, functions, loops — these are the building blocks of every AI application you'll ever write.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Build AI apps">
    Apply everything you've learned to create real AI-powered applications. By the end of the course you'll be calling LLM APIs, processing data with Python, and shipping projects you can actually show off.
  </Step>
</Steps>
